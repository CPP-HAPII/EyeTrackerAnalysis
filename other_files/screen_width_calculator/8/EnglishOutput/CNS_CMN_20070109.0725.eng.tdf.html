January 9, 2007 12:08Chinanews, January 9 A report in the Chongqing Evening News says that next season's coaching line-up for Chongqing Lifan has been officially confirmed. Chongqing Lifan will hold a press conference at their Yanghe base today, announcing that Wei Xin has officially become the head coach, and that Lifan will compete in next season's Chinese Jia League under the guidance of him and two young coaches.Wei Xin, who was a temporary head coach twice last year, appeared quite calm about this official appointment. "All through this period I've been doing the work of the head coach, and the club talked to me about this issue a long time ago.I think the atmosphere in the team this year is extremely good. Everyone is pulling together with the same goal to achieve success. The club's and the team's support for me has been key in my decision to change jobs." Wei Xin also joked that "actually, I haven't got my certificate of appointment yet - in the new season I'll still have to sit on, ah, the players' bench." (Luo Xiang)
