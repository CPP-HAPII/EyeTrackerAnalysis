AFP on the 12th, NairobiPolitical and diplomatic sources said that Kenyan President Kibaki and opposition leader Odinga, the would-be prime minister, have reached an agreement on forming a coalition government and will announce the name list of a new cabinet tomorrow.Sources close to the two leaders said, "The two leaders held talks today and agreed to form a new coalition cabinet. The name list of the cabinet will be announced around noontime tomorrow."A Western diplomat has confirmed that the two parties reached this agreement.The result of Kenya's elections in December of last year led to disputes and violence, leaving at least 1,500 people dead and hundreds of thousands of people displaced.The government and the opposition party later signed a power-sharing agreement in February of this year in an effort to end conflicts.
