2008-04-29 06:46:19 Source: Xinhua News Agency rough draftBeijing (Xinhua News Agency) Olympics special feed, April 28th - 30 exemplary students from Chinese universities will have an opportunity to participate in the reporting of the 2008 Olympics and Special Olympics.On the 28th, the International Olympic Committee global partner, Samsung, announced they will select 30 exemplary university students from Chinese universities to report on the Beijing Olympics and Special Olympics as "Samsung Anycall Reporter."According to the description, the selection process will start in the beginning of May.The selected University student reporters will get an opportunity to attend and report on the Beijing Olympics and Special Olympics.
