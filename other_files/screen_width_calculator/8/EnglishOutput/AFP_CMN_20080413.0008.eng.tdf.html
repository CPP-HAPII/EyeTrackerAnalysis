Harare, capital of Zimbabwe (AFP), 13th.Zimbabwe's state-run media "The Sunday Mail" reported, the Electoral Commission already ordered 23 constituencies to do a comprehensive recount of the ballots before the 19th.Zimbabwe has a total of 210 constituencies.The Sunday Mail, regarded as President Mugabe's government's mouthpiece, reported, the Electoral Commission's chairman Chiweshe already ordered a recount of all ballots for the election of the president, parliament, senate and local legislature.The report said Chiweshe has "reasonable cause" to believe the ballots of these constituencies were miscounted and also probably affected the election results.
