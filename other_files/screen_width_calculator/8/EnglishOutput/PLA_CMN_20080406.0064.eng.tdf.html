2008-04-06 06:24:38 Source: PLA Daily, Page 4Zhurova completed passing the sacred flame with the 79th torch bearer Tian Erlong, consulate general of the Chinese Embassy in St. Petersburg, at the navy department near the Winter Palace and quickly ran forward to the center of the square where cheers and applause rang out from the crowd.Soon after, a grand celebration ceremony and performances were held in the square.St. Petersburg is the third leg for the Beijing Olympics torch relay outside of the country. Before this, the torch was successfully passed in Kazakhstan's Almaty and Turkey's Istanbul. On the afternoon of the 5th, the torch will set off from St. Petersburg for London in the UK, where the torch will start the fourth leg of the relay on the 6th.
