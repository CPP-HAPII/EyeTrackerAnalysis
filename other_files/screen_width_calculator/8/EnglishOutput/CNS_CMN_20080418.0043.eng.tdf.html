April 18, 2008, 12:45, Source: Chinanews.com - CommentChinanews.com, April 18Today is the last day to register for the DPP chairmanship election.According to a report by "Central News Agency," strongly persuaded by many people in the party, former "vice premier of the Executive Yuan" Tsai Ing-wen, after thinking for a long time, has decided to go to the DPP party headquarters in the afternoon to register as a candidate in the election.The deadline of registration for the DPP chairmanship election is 5 o'clock this afternoon.At present, DPP "legislator" Tsai Tung-jung and former "senior presidential adviser" Koo Kwang-ming have completed their registration as candidates in the election.DPP caucus convener Ke Chien-min and DPP "legislator" Gao Jyh-peng had obtained the form for Tsai Ing-wen this morning. Tsai Ing-wen is expected to register as a candidate in the election in the afternoon.
