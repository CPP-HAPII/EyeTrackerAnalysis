January 24, 2007 12:26China News Service, Taipei, January 23 (Reporters Huang Shaohua, Lu Mei). Statistics showed that Taiwan's total volume of export order last year was US $299.313 billion, thus setting another record high of past years with an increase of 16.74 percent from the year before.Among which, electronics and information communications products were still the top two ordered goods. Orders received from Hong Kong and the mainland experienced the highest growth rate.The Ministry of Economic Affairs in Taiwan also announced today that the amount of export orders for last December was US $26.389 billion, an increase of 7.32 percent compared to the same period the year before. Among which, the amount of orders for information and communications products increased by US $900 million and 16.85 percent compared to the same period the year before. Electronic products came next with an increase of US $460 million, representing an 8.36 percent increase.It was said that even though factors including the increase in international oil prices and the macro-control of the mainland existed last year, under the condition of continuous global economic expansion, Taiwan's export grew strongly and orders received performed splendidly. The amount of orders received from Asian regions had a particularly great increase of US $20.51 billion from the year before with an increase rate of 15.79 percent, which was the highest among all.In regards to the major ordering regions, the United States was still the largest ordering market of Taiwan last year, with an order amount reaching US $79.333 billion and an annual increase of 15.1 percent. The order amount from Hong Kong (China included) was US $75.464 billion, representing a growth rate of 24.66 percent and the highest among all regions. The order amount from Europe last year was US $51.207 billion, an increase of 22.28 percent and the third consecutive year to have an increase of over 20 percent.While last year, Taiwan only had a 4.24 percent growth rate in orders received from Japan and an order amount of US $30.852 billion, which marked the lowest increase rate in five years.An official said that the division of labor situation between Taiwan and Japan had changed and orders from Japan experienced an overall decrease in all categories of products.
