2008-04-29 10:22:48 Source: Xinhua News Agency DispatchXinhua News Agency, London, April 28, Olympics Special WireEnglish Championship club West Bromwich drew 1-1 with Southampton in a Championship match on the 28th, thus taking themselves to the top of the table on a score of 78 points level with Stoke City and ranking 1st with a superior goal difference.Two years after bidding farewell to the Premier League, they are finally going to return to the top league.After this round of matches, there will be only one round of league matches left. With a 3-point lead over third-placed Hull City and 12 more goals over Hull City, West Bromwich's chance of losing in the last round and failing to be promoted would exist only in theory.West Bromwich were relegated in 2006 to the Championship league and lost in the Premiership promotion playoff final to Derby County at the end of last season, missing regretfully the chance for promotion.This season the club finally lived up to the fans' expectation and smoothly ascended to the Premier League.Charlton club, where Chinese player Zheng Zhi is, is currently only placed 12th and has long lost hope of promotion.
