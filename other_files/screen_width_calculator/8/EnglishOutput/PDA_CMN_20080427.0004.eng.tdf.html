April 27, 2008People.com.cn, April 27thAccording to a story from China Review News, on the 26th, KMT chairman, Wu Poh-hsiung, stressed that he looks at the issue of whether or not he will make a trip to the continent after Taiwan area's leadership handover ceremony on May 20th with a very solemn frame of mind. It's not going for a leisure trip and he will not go to the Mainland just for the sake of going to the Mainland.Wu Poh-hsiung said, "if it is helpful for the development of cross-strait peace, actually improves the cross-strait atmosphere and is helpful for the welfare of Taiwanese people, as the ruling party, we can make a trip."
