January 28, 2007 22:22File Photo of Zhang XuezhongChina News Service, Photo by Lu YanChinanews, January 28 News from Xinhua Online says that a decision was made on January 28 at the Fifth Session of the Tenth Sichuan Provincial People's Congress to accept the request of Zhang Xuezhong to resign as chairman of the Standing Committee of Sichuan Provincial People's Congress.Zhang Xuezhong was born in 1943 and is a native of Lanzhou, Gansu. He joined the CPC in December 1960.He graduated from the Adult Education School of Lanzhou University in 1966 with an associate degree in Chinese language and literature.He joined the workforce in July 1961 and worked in succession as a teacher in an elementary school affiliated with Lanzhou Teachers' School; secretary at the secretariat of the Gansu Civil Defense Office; secretary at the secretariat of the General Office of the Gansu Provincial Committee; secretary of the CPC Committee of Fafang People's Commune, Wuwei County; secretary of the Yuzhong County Committee; assistant secretary of the Lanzhou Municipal Committee; and secretary of the Longnan Prefectural Committee. After 1989, he served as the vice governor of Gansu Province. After 1990, he served as the assistant secretary of the CPC Tibet Autonomous Regional Committee. After 1994, he took up the posts as the deputy minister, as well as member and assistant secretary of the Leading Party Group, of the Ministry of Personnel. Between June and December 1999, he held a concurrent post as member on the Large-Scale Enterprise Work Committee of the CPC Central Committee. In November 2000, he became the secretary of the Leading Party Group of the CPC Ministry of Personnel, and on December 28, 2000, he was appointed as minister of personnel at the 19th Session of the 9th National People's Congress.In December 2002, he served as a member, a Standing Committee member, and secretary of the CPC Sichuan Provincial Committee.In January 2003, he was elected as chairman of the Standing Committee of the Sichuan Provincial People's Congress at the First session of the Tenth Sichuan Provincial People's Congress.He was a member of the 16th CPC Central Committee.
