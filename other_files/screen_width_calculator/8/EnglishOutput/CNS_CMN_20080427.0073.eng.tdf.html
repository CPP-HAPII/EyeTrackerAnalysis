April 27, 2008, 14:51, Source: Chinanews.com - CommentChinanews.com, April 27 - "Premier-designate" Liu Chao-shiuan will unveil the second name list of the "cabinet" on the 28th. Key posts such as "education minister" and "finance minister" that are receiving close attention are expected to be all unveiled.Liu Chao-shiuan will gather relevant individuals in the evening to make the final decision regarding the second name list of the "cabinet."According to a report by "Central News Agency," Ma Ying-jeou will conclude his trip to Kaohsiung and return to Taipei in the evening. Whether he will discuss the second name list of the "cabinet" with Liu Chao-shiuan is also drawing attention.On the 21st, Liu Chao-shiuan unveiled the first name list of the "cabinet members," making public the 17 names of "cabinet members" such as "vice premier of the Executive Yuan," "secretary-general," "internal affairs," "foreign affairs," "economics," and "judicial ministers." The second name list of the "cabinet" will be unveiled tomorrow.Since the current "Executive Yuan" has a total of 36 ministries and commissions, whether the remaining 19 "ministries and commissions" will be unveiled in full tomorrow will be given final consideration in the evening. More important "ministries and commissions" are expected to be unveiled tomorrow.
